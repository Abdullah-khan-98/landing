What is Blockchain? A blockchain, originally block chain, is a growing list of records, called blocks, that are linked using cryptography. Each block contains a cryptographic hash of the previous block, a timestamp, and transaction data.

Why is Blockchain important more than ever?

This new technology is very promising with implications that go far beyond cryptocurrencies, it is potentially disruptive for dozens of industries and it has already attracted millions of dollars of investment with numerous projects under development.
